Our Heritage

A Legacy of Craftsmanship

Since 1974, Chaudhary Sons & Co. has been synonymous with unparalleled artistry in metal handicrafts. What began as a small workshop has blossomed into a globally recognized name, driven by a passion for preserving traditional techniques while embracing modern design aesthetics.

Every piece we create is a testament to the skill and dedication of our master artisans — objects meant not just to be owned, but to be cherished across generations.
