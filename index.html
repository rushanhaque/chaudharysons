Our Story

The Journey Since 1974

Choudhry Sons Exports was founded on a singular vision: to bring the rich tradition of Indian metalworking to the global stage. From a small artisan workshop in Moradabad, we have grown into an internationally respected name — renowned for quality, authenticity and design.

For generations our family has worked alongside master craftsmen, passing down techniques impossible to replicate by machine. Every curve we hammer and every pattern we engrave is a continuation of a story that is centuries old.
