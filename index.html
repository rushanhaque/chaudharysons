Our Story

The Journey Since 1974

Choudhry Sons Exports was founded on a singular vision: to bring the rich tradition of Indian metalworking to the global stage. From our humble beginnings in a small artisan workshop, we have grown into an internationally respected name, renowned for our commitment to quality, authenticity, and design.

For generations, our family has worked alongside master craftsmen, passing down techniques that are simply impossible to replicate with machinery. Every curve we hammer, every pattern we engrave, and every finish we polish is a continuation of a story that is centuries old.

Today, our products grace homes, hotels, and galleries across more than 30 countries. Yet, our core philosophy remains unchanged: to create art that functions, and objects that endure.
