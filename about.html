A Legacy Forged in Metal

Five decades of heirloom craftsmanship — where ancient technique meets timeless design.

"We do not manufacture objects. We continue a story that is centuries old — one hammer-strike at a time."

Choudhry Sons Exports was founded in 1974 with a singular vision: to bring the rich tradition of Indian metalworking to the global stage. From a modest artisan workshop in Moradabad — the storied brass city of India — we have grown into an internationally respected maison, renowned for our uncompromising commitment to quality, authenticity and design.

Today our pieces grace homes, hotels and galleries across more than thirty countries. Yet our philosophy remains unchanged: to create art that functions, and objects that endure for generations.

The Family Behind the Craft

Three Generations, One Standard

For three generations, the Choudhry family has worked shoulder-to-shoulder with master craftsmen — many of whom learned their art from their own fathers. These are skills impossible to replicate with machinery: the intuition of the hand, the memory in the metal.

Every curve we hammer, every pattern we engrave, and every finish we polish is a continuation of an unbroken lineage of excellence. It is this human touch that makes each piece truly one of a kind.
