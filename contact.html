Good to Know

Frequently Asked

Do you accept bespoke and custom orders?
Absolutely. Bespoke commissions are at the heart of our atelier. Share your concept, dimensions and finish preferences via the form above or our quote page, and our master artisans will craft it to specification.
What is the minimum order for wholesale?
Minimums vary by piece and finish. We work with boutiques, designers and large hospitality groups alike. Tell us your requirements and we'll tailor a quote and lead time for you.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes — we have exported to over thirty countries since 1974. Each piece is professionally packed for safe transit, with full export documentation handled by our team.
What materials and finishes are available?
We specialise in brass, copper and silver/white-metal, offered in polished, brushed, lacquered, antiqued and patinated finishes. We're happy to send physical samples for trade clients.
How long does an order take?
Stocked pieces typically dispatch within 1–2 weeks. Bespoke commissions and large volumes are quoted with a bespoke lead time, usually 4–8 weeks depending on complexity.
